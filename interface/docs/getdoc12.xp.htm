Subject:Re: VPICD_Phys_EOI(hIRQ) and/or VPICD_Physically_Unmask(hIRQ)?
Date:12/03/1999
Author: Christopher Doran <news@cdoran.net>

<< previous in search  ·  next in search >>

Unmask enables interrupts (does not mask), allowing your device to trip the interrupt latches on the PIC (Programmable Interrupt Controller).
 
EOI (End Of Interrupt) then clears the interrupt latches on the PIC during interrupt processing.
 
Christopher Doran
